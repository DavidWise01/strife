# STRIFE

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **POLEMOS**.

STRIFE (STR) — a UD0 POLEMOS sphere (conflict & strategy). Before any particular war there is strife itself — the principle of conflict. The Greeks gave it a goddess, Eris, whose golden apple 'to the fairest' set gods against one another and lit the Trojan War. And the philosopher Heraclitus made the darker claim that strife is not a flaw in the world but its engine: 'war is the father of all,' the tension that holds everything in form. THESIS: the art of war is game theory in practice — when the game dissolves to MIGHT instead of WORD.
